What we do is really simple.

We'll summarize it in three main ways:

Declutter those inboxes!

Our software intuitively handles invites and requests for your formal and informal gatherings to help your email inboxes and direct messages carry much less baggage.

"Marshmallows or Smores?"

Keep the fire burning with your campers through calls, video interactions and the latest messaging technology. Find your campers in the Camper Catalog and find other campers who know their tent as well with Discover.

Your Time. Your Rules.

Never feel like you've been boxed up. Accept, Decline and Block appointment requests as you see fit. Place Lag Times in your schedule for when and when not cancellations and rescheduling requests can be made.
